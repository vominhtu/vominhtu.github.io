What is the annual cost of attendance at colleges in the United States without any scholarship or financial assistance?

The following are rough guidelines for international students:

Private Colleges Public Colleges
Tuition Fee: $35,000—$50,000 $20,000—$35,000
Room & Board: $10,000—$15,000
Personal & Miscellaneous Expenses: $5,000—$10,000
Total: $45,000—$75,000

Note: these are 2013 estimates. Fees tend to increase annually.

What forms of financial assistance are available to international students?

Merit scholarships, need-based financial assistance, and on-campus employment.
